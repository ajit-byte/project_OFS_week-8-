Stakeholders for OFS:

| Stakeholder       | Role                     | Needs/Expectations                          |
|------------------|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Customer          | Orders food             | Easy navigation, secure payment, real-time order status |
| Restaurant Owner  | Prepares food           | Receive orders in real-time, manage menu efficiently |
| Delivery Agent    | Delivers orders         | View assigned orders, track delivery progress |
| Admin             | Manages system          | Manage users, restaurants, and overall system control |
