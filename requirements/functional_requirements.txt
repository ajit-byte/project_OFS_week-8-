Functional Requirements for OFS:

1. The system shall allow users to register and log in securely.
2. The system shall allow customers to browse restaurant menus.
3. The system shall allow customers to add items to a shopping cart.
4. The system shall allow customers to make secure online payments.
5. The system shall send real-time orders to the restaurant for preparation.
6. The system shall allow delivery agents to view their assigned orders.
7. The system shall allow admins to manage user accounts and restaurant data.
8. The system shall provide real-time updates on order status to users.
