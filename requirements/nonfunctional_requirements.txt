Non-Functional Requirements for OFS:

1. The system shall be accessible 24/7 with minimal downtime.
2. Payments shall be processed securely using SSL/TLS encryption.
3. The system shall respond to user actions within 2 seconds on average.
4. The user interface shall be responsive and compatible with mobile and desktop devices.
5. Order updates shall be reflected in real-time.
6. The system shall be able to handle at least 100 concurrent users.
